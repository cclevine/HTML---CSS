      <h2 id="main">Checkpoints</h2>
      <p>The checkpoints below will guide me on my path to complete "The Great Loop"</p>
      <p>"The Great Loop" starts in Denver and ends in Denver.</p>

      		<p id="pagenav">

	    <ol>
	      <li><h3 id="denver">Denver</h3></li>
			<p>The trip starts out in Denver.  Make sure you have all your gear</p>
			<p>and your bike is in good shape before you leave.</p>
			<p>Here is a good <a href="www.coloradopowersports.com">motorcycle repair shop</a> in Denver.</p>
			<p>Load up on gear and get your bike in touring condition!</p>
	      	<p class="toplink"><a href="#skipnav">Back to top</a></p>
	      <li><h3 id="alamosa">Alamosa National Wildlife Refuge</h3></li>
			<p><a href="http://www.fws.gov/alamosa/">Alamosa National Wildlife Refuge</a> is full of great things to see.</p>
			<p>If you're lucky, you may even spot a Bobcat!</p>
	      	<p class="toplink"><a href="#skipnav">Back to top</a></p>
	      <li><h3 id="durango">Durango</h3></li>
			<p>Stop for a nice hike in <a href="http://www.durango.org/">Durango.</a></p>
			<p>You will find some of the most picturesque views in this quintisential Colorado town!</p>
	      	<p class="toplink"><a href="#skipnav">Back to top</a></p>
	      <li><h3 id="arches">Arches National Park</h3></li>
			<p>About an hour outside Colorado is <a href="http://www.discovermoab.com/archesnationalpark.htm">Arches National Park.</a></p>
			<p>This park contains the world's largest concentration of natural sandstone arches.</p>
	    	<p class="toplink"><a href="#skipnav">Back to top</a></p>
		  <li><h3 id="rocky">Rocky Mountain National Park</h3></li>
			<p>On the way back to Denver, be sure to stop off at <a href="http://www.nps.gov/romo/index.htm">Rocky Mountain National Park.</a></p>
			<p>Rocky Mountain National Park is one of our nations most famous parks and it's only about an hour and a half from Denver!</p>
