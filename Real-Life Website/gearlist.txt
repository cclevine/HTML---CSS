      <h2 id="main">Gearlist</h2>
		<p>In order to have a truely amazing and successful motorcycle adventure, planning you gear is of paramount importance.</p>

		<p>You must make sure your bike is in sound mechanical condition so you dont have to worry about breaking down and for safety purposes.</p>
